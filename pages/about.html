I have been attending Northeastern University since fall of 2012, and am a Digital Art and Game Design combined major who plans to concentrate in Animation.

I am thoroughly enjoying the challenges and excitement that Northeastern continues to throw my way, and can't wait to get involved even more!

I enjoy anime, animation, video games, computers, good food, and good music. I like to appreciate what's given to me in life, do my best when I'm in a sticky situation, and work diligently to achieve my goals.

Sometimes, I like to pretend I'm as wise as a sage, giving what I deem as good advice to others. For me, giving advice is a pretty fun time because I have fun helping people if I can be of assistance to them. Volunteering is also something that is of interest to me, because it's another form of helping others that lets me know that I helping to repair the world.

Some places in the world that I'd like to visit are Israel, Japan, and Finland, if I ever get the chance to.

If you've read this far, one last thing I'd like to say, is: please enjoy my portfolio!
